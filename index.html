Privacy Policy — Quant Evolution

Last updated: April 22, 2026 · Sviatoslav Parashchak

Overview

Quant Evolution is a free arcade game developed by Sviatoslav Parashchak. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how you can control it. We collect only what is necessary to provide the leaderboard and account features.

Data We Collect

When you create an account, the following data is collected and stored in Google Firebase (Firestore & Authentication):

Data Purpose Storage
Email address Account authentication Firebase Auth
Nickname Displayed on leaderboard Firestore
Best score Global leaderboard ranking Firestore
High score (local) Personal best, offline play Device only

Account creation is optional. You can play the game fully without registering — your local high score is saved on your device only and never uploaded without your consent.

How Data Is Used

Your data is used exclusively to:

— Authenticate your account and keep you signed in
— Display your nickname and score on the global leaderboard
— Sync your personal best across sessions

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising or marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

We use Google Firebase (by Google LLC) for authentication and data storage. Firebase processes data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections.

No analytics, advertising SDKs, or other third-party services are used in this app.

Account Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time directly from within the game:

  1. Open the game and go to the Main Menu
  2. Tap the Login / Account button
  3. In the account screen, tap Delete account
  4. Confirm — your email, nickname, and scores are permanently deleted
Note: If you have not signed in recently, you may need to sign out and sign back in before deleting your account (Firebase security requirement). Deletion is immediate and irreversible.

After deletion, the following data is permanently removed from Firebase: your email address, nickname, and all leaderboard scores linked to your account.

Children's Privacy

Quant Evolution is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. Account creation requires an email address — parents or guardians are responsible for supervising account creation by minors. If you believe a child has created an account without parental consent, contact us at the email below and we will delete it immediately.

Data Retention

Your account data is retained for as long as your account exists. If you delete your account, all associated data is removed immediately. Local high score data stored on your device can be cleared by uninstalling the app.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
